Defa-protector-protect-html5-video-from-download

Defa Protector is a php script that protect html5 video on your website from being downloaded. The project aims to give a safe video protection all over the world. Frequently asked question : Defa Protector doesn’t send you a real video files which make some browser video player cannot load the video but we support most of the modern browser

Download this project as a .zip file Download this project as a tar.gz file

Welcome to Defa Protector.

Defa Protector is a video and music download protection software based on Computer Networks theory and Written in PHP. It mostly can be run on Normal Web Server , Shared Hosting and in many environment without need of additional plugin. Unlike, Many Paid Video Protection Software.

Defa Protector also able to let you protect your video while user can play video directly without need to install additional plugin on Client Side.

Defa Protector can protect video download in very high percentage but it isn't 100% and doesn't have warranty.

Installation and Usage

For Wordpress Plugin :

By Installing and Activating Plugin. The Software will works automatically and you likely to see a help page on WP-ADMIN

For ALL CMS :

Finding your CMS PHP page that output your content. usually, It was index.php

Include includetop on top of the page

include('includetop.php');

and include includebottom.php on the bottom of the page

include('includebottom.php');

and test it by watching the video and downloading it or right click on video url and select copy video url

Paste on your browser . if it contains defavid.php on url address then it works

Usage HTML5 :

Defa Protector will automatically bind video , audio , source tag. It works with HTML5 but not design to work with Flash

Defa Protector can or cannot protect flash video because it doesn't design for and It won't protect iframe tag.
